‚ùì Frequently Asked Questions

Why doesn't my alarm go off when my phone is locked?

Peaceful Wake Up automatically prevents your phone from locking when an alarm is set. If you're experiencing issues, make sure the app has permission to run in the background and that Low Power Mode is disabled.

Can I use the app without an internet connection?

Yes! Peaceful Wake Up works completely offline. All features, including alarms, sunrise simulation, and audio playback, function without an internet connection.

Why is my screen so bright during the sunrise phase?

This is intentional! The app automatically increases your device brightness to maximum during the sunrise simulation to create an effective wake-up light. Your original brightness is restored after the alarm.

Does the app work if my phone is on silent mode?

Yes, unless you choose "Silent Alarm." The app can override silent mode to play gentle wake-up sounds. If you prefer a completely silent experience, enable the "Silent Alarm" option.

What data does the app collect?

None! Peaceful Wake Up stores only your alarm settings locally on your device. No data is transmitted to servers or shared with third parties. See our Privacy Policy for complete details.

Can I set multiple alarms or recurring alarms?

Currently, Peaceful Wake Up supports one alarm at a time. Multiple alarm support and recurring alarms are planned for future updates.

üîß Troubleshooting

Alarm Not Going Off

Step 1: Ensure the alarm is properly set by checking that the "Cancel Alarm" button is visible and the countdown timer shows time remaining.
Step 2: Make sure your device is not in Low Power Mode, as this can affect background app refresh and timers.
Step 3: Check that Background App Refresh is enabled for Peaceful Wake Up in Settings > General > Background App Refresh.
Step 4: Restart the app and set the alarm again. Force-close the app by swiping up and swiping away, then reopen.

Audio Issues

Volume Too Low: The app starts audio at 10% and gradually increases. If you need louder initial volume, adjust your device's volume buttons during the alarm.
No Sound: Check if "Silent Alarm" is enabled. If you want sound, uncheck this option when setting your alarm.
Audio Interrupted: Other apps may interrupt audio playback. Close competing audio apps or restart Peaceful Wake Up.

Screen Brightness Issues

Screen Too Bright: The sunrise simulation intentionally maximizes brightness. Your original brightness setting is automatically restored when you cancel the alarm.
Screen Too Dim: If Auto-Brightness is interfering, temporarily disable it in Settings > Display & Brightness during alarm setup.

Performance Issues

App Crashes: Force-close the app, restart your device, and reopen Peaceful Wake Up. If issues persist, try reinstalling the app.
Slow Performance: Close other running apps to free up memory. The app includes built-in performance optimizations for smooth operation.

‚ú® Feature Guide

Sunrise Simulation

The app creates a natural wake-up experience by gradually increasing screen brightness over 10 minutes before your alarm time. This mimics a natural sunrise and helps regulate your circadian rhythm.

Smart Volume Control

Audio starts at a gentle 10% volume and increases to 100% over 3 minutes. This prevents jarring wake-ups while ensuring you don't oversleep.

Inactivity Management

The screen automatically dims after 30 seconds of inactivity to save battery, but stays bright during the sunrise phase. Tap anywhere to restore full brightness.

Privacy Protection

All alarm settings are stored locally on your device using secure iOS UserDefaults. No data is transmitted to external servers or shared with third parties.
